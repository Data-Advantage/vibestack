## 4.4 SEO Content Plan

**Goal**: Generate a comprehensive SEO strategy to improve your application's visibility in search engines.

**Process**: Follow this chat pattern with an AI chat tool such as [Claude.ai](https://www.claude.ai). Pay attention to the notes in `[brackets]` and replace the bracketed text with your own content.

**Timeframe**: 30-45 minutes

### 4.4.1: Keyword Research & Opportunity Analysis

```
I need to develop a comprehensive SEO content strategy for my SaaS application. I want to identify the most valuable keywords and content opportunities that will help potential customers find our product.

Please paste your relevant documents below to provide context:

<product-requirements-document>
[Paste your PRD from step 1.1 here]
</product-requirements-document>

<value-proposition>
[Paste your value proposition document from step 4.2 here]
</value-proposition>

<feature-highlights>
[Paste your feature highlights from step 4.3 here, if available]
</feature-highlights>

Based on these documents, please help me:

1. Identify 3-4 primary keyword categories relevant to our product:
   - Core product/service keywords
   - Problem-based keywords (what issues users are trying to solve)
   - Solution-based keywords (specific solutions users are searching for)
   - Comparative keywords (vs. alternatives or competitors)

2. For each category, suggest:
   - 5-7 high-value target keywords/phrases
   - Search intent behind these keywords
   - Estimated competition level
   - Content types that would best serve this intent

3. Identify potential content gaps and opportunities:
   - Underserved search intents in our market
   - Question-based queries we could address
   - Long-tail opportunities with lower competition
   - Emerging trends or topics in our space

Please provide this analysis in a structured format that will help me develop a targeted SEO content strategy.
```

- Priority order for content creation

## 4. Content Distribution Channels
- Primary platforms for content promotion

Please provide a comprehensive framework that will guide our ongoing content development efforts.
